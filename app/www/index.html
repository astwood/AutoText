Welcome to AutoText - the ultimate app to schedule text messages - to yourself, friends or groups. AutoText allows you compose your text and them simply schedule it for when you would like it to be sent - this message will then be sent for you regardless of whether your phone has signal, or is even switched on!

Before you get started we'd just like to take you through the key features of AutoText.

For your selected home country, , it costs credit(s) to send 1 text message to 1 recipient. For the cost to send 1 message to 1 recipient to any of our supported countries please see below. Just like standard SMS messages, if you send to multiple recipients or send a long message this will require multiple credits.

For each scheduled message we break down for you the number of messages your text requires and the number of recipients. If all the recipients are local numbers then it's simple: Number of messages x number of recipients x credit cost = total credits to send message.
